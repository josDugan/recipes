Crown Royal Manhattan up version 1

6 parts Crown Royal canadian whiskey
1 part sweet red vermouth
2 t0 3 dashes of blood orange bitters
1 maraschino cherry

1.  Chill a martini glass by adding ice and filling with water.
2.  Fill a mixing glass with ice.
3.  Add the whiskey.
4.  Add the vermouth.
5.  Add the bitters.
6.  Stir the contents of the mixing glass for approximately 30 seconds.
7.  Empty the pre-chilled martini glass.
8.  Strain the manhattan into the martini glass; prevent any ice from falling into the glass.
9.  Drop the cherry into the glass.
10.  Serve or enjoy.
